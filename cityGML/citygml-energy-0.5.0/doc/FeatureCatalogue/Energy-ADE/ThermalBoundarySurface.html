Feature type: ThermalBoundarySurface

Package:

Energy-ADE

Definition:

Thermal boundary surfaces are quasi-coplanar surfaces bounding the thermal zone. They may be linked to the gml:BoundarySurface when possible, but not necessary (e.g. cellar ceiling or top storey ceiling in the case of LOD1-3)
